Smart Study Lamp

While in my dorm room, struggling with the on/off button of my desk lamp, I pondered a solution. Could I automate this process and gather study time data to track and enhance my academic performance?

The CAD desgin for the lamp

How it work:

It works by connecting an Arduino to an LED lightbulb via a 5V relay. I encased it in a 3D printed enclosure and connected the USB wire to my laptop. The bulb can automatically turn on and off based on whether or not I am studying, thanks to a facial recognition algorithm that I created. The algorithm also extracts data to illustrate the length of a user's study session.

In the future, I intended to switch from LED lightbulbs to LED strips for a more modular design. I also intended to rebuild the chassis to provide a more rapid and simple fix.
