Smart Study Lamp

I was in my dorm room when I saw that I was having a really hard time pressing the on and off button of my desklamp. I really like my desklamp and I thought is there a way I can automate the on/off switch of the lamp so that I don't have to care about it and also give me some useful data of my study time to help my better at my academic.

The CAD desgin for the lamp

How it work:

It work by connecting an arduino to a LED lightbulb through a 5V relay. Using a face recognition program that I create, the lamp can automatically turn on and off by noticing whether I am studying or not. The code also extract data to show how long is a user's study session.
